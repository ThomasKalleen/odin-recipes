Air Fryer Steak and Mushrooms

Yeah, you know you've been there.

Description

"But why," you ask, looking to the sky as if the heavens themselves would offer you an answer, "would someone need instructions on how to cook instant ramen in the first place?"

"Because," Hell answers, "if they want instant ramen, chances are things aren't going great so they need all the help they can get."

"Sounds like you speak from experience, Mr. Hell."

"I don't want to talk about it. I LEFT IT ALL BEHIND ME."

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Kraft Dinner
  • 2 tablespoons of cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 20g of cheese chunks

Steps

  1. Boil water. Toss the Kraft Dinner inside of it. Cook and stir for 7 to 8 minutes.
  2. Drain the kraft dinner, then return it to the pan.
  3. Add butter, cream, and cheese sauce from inside the KD box to it. Also add the extra cheese chunks. Mix it.
  4. It is now done, may god have mercy on your soul.
